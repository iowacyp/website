Iowa National Guard Child & Youth Program — Typography Spec
Updated: 2025-10-14

Primary Headline Font: League Spartan
 - Weights: 600, 700
 - Use for: H1–H3, buttons, badges, short callouts
 - Source: Google Fonts

Body Font: Source Sans 3
 - Weights: 400, 600 (optional)
 - Use for: paragraph text, UI labels, captions
 - Source: Google Fonts

Color Tokens:
 - CYP Red: #cb3d4e
 - CYP Blue: #012d6b

Web Implementation:
 - Include branding_fonts.css, or copy into your design system
 - Use .text-red/.text-blue for quick accents
 - Headings are uppercase by default for brand consistency

Accessibility Notes:
 - Aim for 4.5:1 contrast for body text, 3:1 for large headings
 - Avoid placing red text on blue backgrounds (and vice versa) for readability
